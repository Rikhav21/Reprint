Reprint YSWS

This is a YSWS where you can redesign some "suboptimal" printers and get the grant to make it yourself! To get started you can take a look at the available printers. Either you can can choose one of these from aliexpress, or you can find any printer less than $80 from facebook marketplace, ebay, etc.Once you have your printer, you can start redesigning it! You can use any CAD software you want, but if this your your first experience with CAD, I would recommend using Fusion 360 or onshape.
